# Product Overview

Newon is a comprehensive inventory management system designed to streamline business operations with multi-brand support. The system handles the complete lifecycle of inventory, sales, and financial tracking for retail/wholesale businesses.

## Core Capabilities

- Inventory management with variants, attributes, and multi-location stock tracking
- Purchase order management with supplier tracking
- Customer relationship management with transaction history
- Invoice and quotation generation with PDF export
- Project-based expense tracking and budget management
- Financial ledger with payment tracking
- Virtual products for service-based items
- Multi-brand support (Newon, Waymor)
- Role-based access control (Admin/Staff)

## Key Business Entities

- Products: Physical inventory items with variants, pricing, and stock levels
- Virtual Products: Service-based items without physical inventory
- Customers: Client records with financial history and ledger entries
- Projects: Budget-tracked initiatives with expense management
- Invoices/Quotations: Sales documents with payment tracking
- Purchases: Supplier orders and inventory receipts
- Ledger: Financial transaction records per customer
- Staff: User accounts with role-based permissions

## Special Features

- OTC (Over the Counter) customer for walk-in sales
- Auto-generated IDs for all entities (INV-YYYY-XXXX format)
- Cloudinary integration for product images
- PDF generation for invoices and ledger reports
- Real-time budget tracking with over-budget alerts
